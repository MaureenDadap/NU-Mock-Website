Scholarship Grants

Don Mariano F. Jhocson Gold Scholar (With Highest Honors/ May Pinakamataas na Karangalan)

Requirements:
  1. Certificate coming from the high school and signed by the Principal indicating the award received by the student
  2. Original Grade 12 Report Card (for incoming Freshmen)
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character from High School (for incoming Freshmen)
  4. Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  5. Must pass the National University Admissions Test (NUAT) – ABOVE AVERAGE score
  6. Applicants should not have a grade lower than 90 in any subject
Benefits:
  1. 100% Discount on Tuition Fee
  2. 100% Discount on Miscellaneous Fee
  3. Free set of School Uniform
  4. Trimester Book Allowance
  5. Trimestral stipend
Note:

Gold scholars are still subject for interview to qualify for other benefits ( Free School Uniform, Trimester Book Allowance, Trimestral stipend). This can be availed for 4years/5years (depending on the degree program) provided that the student complies with the retention requirements set by the University. Applicants should not have a grade lower than 90 in any subject. The discount is applicable for the term of the entry only.

Mariano Jhocson Science Scholarship Program

Awarded to students from the identified Science high schools whose NUAT score is at least 90% with a HS GWA of at least 88% and an annual family income not exceeding Php360,000.00 per year.

Requirements:
  1. Latest tax exemption certificate or income tax return of both parents
  2. Two (2) recommendation letters from school administrators or faculty (attach photocopy of valid ID of the recommender) addressed to Admissions Director
  3. Original Grade 12 Report Card (for incoming Freshmen)
  4. Certificate of Good Moral Character from High School (for incoming Freshmen)
  5. Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
Benefits:
  1. 100% Discount on Tuition Fee
  2. 100% Discount on Miscellaneous Fee
Note:

The discount is applicable for the term of the entry only.
