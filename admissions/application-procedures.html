Application Procedures

Freshmen

a student who is a high school graduate of a Philippine or international school, college or university and has not been admitted to any collegiate program in another Philippines school, college or university

Qualifications
  1. Completed middle school or its equivalent, e.g. Philippine Educational Test (PEPT), Alternative Learning System (ALS) or a Non-Formal Equivalency test both given and certified by the Department of Education.
  2. Passing the National University Admissions Test (NUAT).
Online Registration Steps:
  1. Applicants must apply online.
  2. Register and apply in the online admission application via this link.
  3. Fill out the NU Application Form.

Transferee Student

a student from another Philippines school, college or university who has been granted Certificate of Transfer Credentials and seeks admissions in any of the collegiate programs of this University.

Qualifications
  1. For degree programs with board exams, transferees are required to pass a higher cut off percentile to get admitted.
  2. The following degree programs does not accept admission of students who intend to take as a second degree or second coursers since this courses can only accept up to second year level as of the moment (new program):
    • BS Medical Technology/ Medical Laboratory Services
    • BS Real Estate Management
    • BS Tourism Management
  3. Passing the National University Admissions Test (NUAT)
Online Registration Steps:
  1. Applicants must apply online.
  2. Register and apply in the online admission application via this link.
  3. Fill out the NU Application Form.

Note: Each successful transaction will be confirmed with an email notification sent to the registered email account. Applicants will be notified through email for the result.

  • Entrance exam is free of charge
  • No retake policy
  • The test results are valid for a period of one (1) year.

Cross-Enrollee

a student officially enrolled in another school, college or university who is permitted to cross-enroll in National University to take a subject that is not, at that moment, offered in his/her school, college or university.

Second Degree Student

a student who is a graduate of a Bachelor’s degree program from a Philippine school, college or university and pursues another Bachelor’s degree program offered in the University.

Graduate School Student

a student who earned a Bachelor’s degree program from a Philippine school, college or university and pursues an advanced academic degree offered within the University.
