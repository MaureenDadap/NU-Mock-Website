College of Education, Arts, and Sciences

Bachelor of Arts in English

Courses and Descriptions

This program prepares students to become resourceful, problem solvers and visionary innovators. They are uniquely prepared for today’s challenges and tomorrow’s opportunities by making them understand how learning takes place in and out of the classroom setting and equips them with the innovations and trends in the English Language in various contexts. It also trains students to conduct researches and expose them to different discourses to realize how the role of language in different sectors operates. And, it empowers students to find answers to their life’s questions through inquiry, dialogue, and analysis.

Bachelor in Elementary Education

Courses and Descriptions

This education program aims to develop the teaching skills of students in educating early age learners. It exposes the students to the modern methods and techniques in teaching the basic knowledge and skills in all subject areas covered by the primary education. In as much as graduates of this course can only teach after having passed the Licensure Examination for Teachers (LET), students are as well prepared for them to pass this examination.

Bachelor in Secondary Education Major in English

Courses and Descriptions

This teacher education program provides the students with principles and up-to-date strategies that they can apply in teaching English as a second language. It prepares the students to be skillful in listening and reading and proficient in speaking and writing to convey knowledge to the students in secondary education in terms of vocabulary, grammar, discourse, and literary works. It is designed to train students to be competent and effective teachers of the 21st century high school learners of English.
